Aragorn

the last surviving heir to the thrones of Arnor and Gondor

Here's a time line of Aragorn's life

  • 2931 of the third ages - Aragorn's born
  • 2933 of the third ages - Aragorn is taken to Imladris. Elrond receives him and names him Estel (Hope), his origins are hidden.
  • 2951 of the third ages - Aragorn receives Anduril and his true identity is revealed
  • 2957-2980 of the third ages - Aragorn undertakes his long journeys and his wanderings. In disguise, he serves Thengel de Rohan and Echtelion II de Gondor.
  • 3001 of the third ages - Start of gollum hunting
  • 3017 of the third ages - End of gollum hunting
  • 3019 of the third ages - War of the Ring, Aragorn founds the Reunified Kingdom and marries Arwen
  • 6 of the fourth ages - Aragorn forbids men from entering the Shire
  • 120 of the fourth ages - Aragorn death
